The following community plugins are installed:
- **Calendar**: Adds a little clickable calendar to your notebook. Used for

	- [[Periodic notes and lab journal]]

	- [[Periodic notes and lab journal]]
- **[Templater](https://silentvoid13.github.io/Templater/)**: The Templater plugin is used to create the templates for all workflows.
- **[Zotero integration](https://github.com/mgmeyers/obsidian-zotero-integration)**: Connect your Zotero library with Obsidian and create literature notes, citations and bibliographies. Used in
	- [[Literature notes from Zotero]]

Of course there are many other cool community plugins. Check out [[Get Started - Resources#ðŸ§© Community plugins]] for some suggestions.
